An Interactive Wine Aroma Wheel

Click on the Segments to zoom-in, and click on the grapes in the center to zoom-out

Introduction:

This is an Interactive Wine Aroma Wheel based on the Davis Wine Aroma Wheel by A.C. Noble.
It is an interactive sunburst chart built using the D3.js JavaSript library, version 3.
